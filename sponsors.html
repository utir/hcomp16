Sponsor HCOMP 2016

  • Sponsorship Overview

Sponsors play a vital role in ensuring HCOMP's continuing success. By keeping registration fees affordable for academic researchers, especially students, sponsorships from corporations, universities, and other institutions help sustain HCOMP's economic viability and proud tradition of strong participation each year.

Whether you want to showcase your technology to the crowdsourcing community or tap into this incredible source of talent, HCOMP is the premier venue for sponsors to reach this unique audience of researchers, industry practioners, & media, combining social scientists and influencers with technological innovators and adopters.

HCOMP is organized under the aegis of AAAI. To become a sponsor, please contact us at hcomp16@aaai.org or telephone +1 (650) 328-3123 (USA).

  • Sponsorship Levels

All sponsors will be acknowedged on the conference website, in printed materials, in opening and closing plenary sessions, and in onsite signage, as available.

HCOMP offers four sponsorship levels: Champions, Benefactors, Contributors, and Friends. We will acknowledge sponsors in order by contribution level.

Champions ($10,000 or greater contribution)

  • Named event, such as a reception, poster session, student event, or industry panel
  • Invitation to present in demo session
  • Complimentary 1/4 black & white ad in AI Magazine
  • Acknowledgement at all social events, including receptions, the conference banquet/party, coffee breaks, and student events
  • 2 complimentary conference registrations, including all social events and main conference sessions
  • Exhibit table
  • Conference packet insert

Benefactors ($7,500 or greater contribution)

  • Invitation to present in demo session
  • Complimentary 1/4 black & white ad in AI Magazine
  • Acknowledgement at all social events, including receptions, the conference banquet/party, coffee breaks, and student events
  • 1 complimentary conference registration, including all social events and main conference sessions
  • Exhibit table
  • Conference packet insert

Contributors ($4,000 or greater contribution)

  • 1 complimentary conference registration, including all social events and main conference sessions
  • Exhibit table or conference packet insert

Friends ($500 or greater contribution)

  • Exhibit table or conference packet insert
