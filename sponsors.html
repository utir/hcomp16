Call for Sponsors

  • Sponsorship Overview

Sponsors play a vital role in ensuring HCOMP's continuing success. By keeping registration fees affordable for academic researchers, especially students, sponsorships from corporations, universities, and other institutions help sustain HCOMP's economic viability and proud tradition of strong participation each year.

Whether you want to showcase your technology to the crowdsourcing community or tap into this incredible source of talent, HCOMP is the premier venue for sponsors to reach this unique audience of researchers, industry practioners, & media, combining social scientists and influencers with technological innovators and adopters.

HCOMP is organized under the aegis of AAAI. To become a sponsor, please contact us at hcomp16@aaai.org or telephone +1 (650) 328-3123 (USA).

  • Press Passes

To increase visibility and publicity for HCOMP presenters and attendees, HCOMP offers a free press pass for credentialed members of the press.

  • Sponsorship Levels

HCOMP offers four sponsorship levels: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Sponsors will be acknolwedged in order by contribution level.

Platinum ($10,000 or greater contribution)

  • Named event (4-6 available), such as a reception, poster session, student event, or industry panel
  • 2 complimentary conference registrations (instead of 1), including all social events and main conference sessions
    • Note: Conference registration was $650-850 per person in 2015 (based on date registered; TBD for 2016)
  • All benefits of lower tier sponsors (see below)

Gold ($7,500 or greater contribution)

  • Complimentary 1/4 black & white ad in AI Magazine
  • Acknowledgement at all social events, including receptions, the conference banquet/party, coffee breaks, and student events
  • Attendee resume book: all resumes will have the option to access company job descriptions and provide resumes directly as part of registration; interviews can be scheduled in advance with sponsoring companies
  • Job descriptions provided to attendees
  • All benefits of lower tier sponsors (see below)

Silver ($4,000 or greater contribution)

  • 1 complimentary conference registration, including all social events and main conference sessions
    • Note: Conference registration was $650-850 per person in 2015 (based on date registered; TBD for 2016)
  • Invitation to Sponsors Dinner
  • Attendee emails: two emails to all HCOMP16 attendees, one before and one after the conference
  • Invitation to exhibit in poster/demo session
  • All benefits of lower tier sponsors (see below)

Bronze ($500 or greater contribution)

  • Conference packet insert
  • Acknowedgement on the conference website, in printed materials, in opening and closing plenary sessions, and in onsite signage, as available.
