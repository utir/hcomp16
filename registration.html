Registration Information

  • HCOMP-16 Registration Information

The 4th AAAI Conference on Human Computation and Crowdsourcing (HCOMP 2016) will be held October 30 - November 3 in Austin, TX (USA) at the AT&T Executive Conference Center, on campus at the University of Texas at Austin.

To register, please use the HCOMP-16 online registration form

October 30: Doctoral Consortium / Tutorial (UT School of Information)
October 31 - November 2: HCOMP-16 Technical Program
November 3: Workshop/CrowdCamp (CrowdCamp Social Activity: evening of November 2)

PREREGISTRATION IS ENCOURAGED. The HCOMP-16 technical conference registration fee includes admission to all technical paper and poster sessions, invited talks, the opening reception, and an electronic copy of the HCOMP-16 Conference Proceedings. Technical registrants also receive a discount on attendance at the workshops on November 3.

  • 2016 HCOMP Proceedings

Preregistrants may order a hard copy of the HCOMP-16 proceedings, which will be mailed after the conference. The preconference discounted price will be determined in fall 2016. Shipping and handling will be added. Individuals who select this option will be contacted regarding the final cost after the conference.

  • Registration / Proof of Student Status

To register online, please complete the form at ( https://www.regonline.com/hcomp16). If you qualify for student rates, you will be required to submit proof of student status (PDF) through the online system before you can complete your registration.

  • Refund Requests

The deadline for refund requests is October 14, 2016. All refund requests must be made in writing to AAAI at hcomp16@aaai.org. A $100.00 processing fee will be assessed for all refunds.

  • Onsite Registration

Preregistration is required prior to arrival onsite by registering online. Onsite registration will be held in the foyer of Salon C on the third level of the AT&T Conference Center at the University of Texas, 1900 University Avenue, Austin Texas, 78705, October 31 - November 3. All attendees must pick up their registration packets for admittance to programs.

Registration hours are scheduled for Monday, October 31, 8:00am - 5:00pm; Tuesday and Wednesday, November 1-2, 8:30am - 5:00pm; and Thursday, November 3, 8:30am - 12:00noon. These hours are subject to change depending on the final program.
