Sponsor HCOMP 2016

  • Benefits of Sponsorship

Two sources of income pay for HCOMP conference expenses: registration fees paid by the attendees and sponsorships from corporations, universities, and other institutions. Sponsorsâ€™ funds allow HCOMP conferences to limit increasing registration, especially for students and the assistance provided by our sponsors is greatly appreciated.

HCOMP will provide benefits to sponsors in exchange for their financial support. To build awareness of sponsorship, we will publically acknowledge all sponsors in the opening and closing plenary sessions, on the conference website, in the proceedings, and final program. We will list the sponsors in order of contribution. We will also acknowledge Champion and Benefactors sponsors for their contribution to well attended social events including receptions, the conference banquet/party, coffee breaks, and student dinners.

Sponsors are given the opportunity to provide information (e.g., brochures) directly to HCOMP attendees. Champion, Benefactor, and Contributor sponsors are invited to present demonstrations of their relevant technology. HCOMP will also accommodate sponsors interested in recruiting conference attendees. Sponsors may also receive a complimentary HCOMP registration.

  • Sponsorship Levels

HCOMP offers four sponsorship levels: Champions, Benefactors, Contributors, and Friends.

Champions ($7,500 or greater contribution)

Benefactors ($5,000 or greater contribution)

Contributors ($2,500 or greater contribution)

Friends (contribution of less than $2,500)
